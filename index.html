My Thoughts on Lifeweaver

Lifeweaver is a support expected to release on April 11th, 2023. This guide is being released just two days before that, so these thoughts are entirely hypothetical. His rate of healing is extremely disappointing. For reference, each shot from Ana's rifle heals a teammate 75 health. Lifeweaver has worse range, has to charge to get to max healing, has only twelve shots to Ana's fifteen, AND it heals only 65 health at max charge.

However, he manages to maintain his extreme utility due to his pull ability to save a teammate and make them briefly immortal, along with his flower petal ability that allows immediate access for himself and teammates to get to highground or create their own. This sets the pace of the game in creative and interesting ways. I think that in lower levels of competition, he will suffer greatly due to that weak healing, but at higher levels of competition with heavy communication, he will be very exciting to watch.
