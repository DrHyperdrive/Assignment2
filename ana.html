Ana - The Support Sniper

About Ana

Ana is a support sniper. Her biotic rifle heals teammates and damages enemies and damages enemies. In a duel against Widowmaker, the DPS sniper of this game, Ana lost her right eye. This is demonstrated in game through the fact that Ana cannot headshot, her healing and damage are exactly 75, regardless of where you hit your teammate or opponent. Understanding this is very important, since it removes the need to be as precise as a DPS sniper. Your primary job is to heal teammates and take shots on the enemy. Since your damage is so consistent, you can get kills more often than most supports. The majority of the cast will always die after three shots.
