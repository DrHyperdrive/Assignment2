Brigitte - The Support Tank

About Brigitte

Brigitte is a support who functions a little bit like a second tank. She has a personal shield, and her primary weapon is a close ranged mace. The most important part about playing Brigitte is her passive, which is Inspire. When Brigitte his an opponent with a primary mace hit or with a whip shot, she procs her Inspire, which heals all teammates around you at a low, but consistant rate. She is definitely a sustain support, and her main utility is being a strong presence and displacing opponents with her whip shot. I tend to switch to Brigitte when I am being heavily targeted while playing Ana, or when my fellow support selects Ana so that I can play a sort of body guard for her.

Brigitte's Abilities

Sleep dart is incredibly useful, as it is one of the remaining forms of true CC in the game since the purge when Overwatch 2 released. The ability to remove an opponent from the match for 5 seconds is unbelievably valuable. This can be used against a tank to dive their backline, to defend yourself when you are being dived, or even to counter ultimates like Reaper's Death Blossom. Landing sleeps will bring your team an incredible amount of value and it is essential that you get familiar with this tool.

Anti-Nade is your most powerful fight-winning tool. You throw a grenade that splashes the enemies and teammates around it. The teammates are healed an instant 100 health and receive bonus healing for 3 seconds, AND the opponents are dealt 60 damage and cannot be healed for 3 seconds. This is incredible for earning kills and assists since the opponents will be greatly softened up for your team to dive. It also has a potential 500 instant healing, and still boosts any more potential healing you may need! Deciding when and how to use this ability is the defining game-decider with Ana.

Nano is Ana's ultimate. It instantly heals the target 250 health, halves incoming damage to that target, and increases their damage dealt by 50%. This ability supercharges a teammate for incredible plays. This is often saved for either the tank or a specific DPS that synergizes well with it (an example is Genji with his ultimate, known by the community as Nanoblade). It can also be used on anyone to give them a second chance at life.
