Brigitte - The Support Tank

About Brigitte

Brigitte is a support who functions a little bit like a second tank. She has a personal shield, and her primary weapon is a close ranged mace. The most important part about playing Brigitte is her passive, which is Inspire. When Brigitte his an opponent with a primary mace hit or with a whip shot, she procs her Inspire, which heals all teammates around you at a low, but consistant rate. She is definitely a sustain support, and her main utility is being a strong presence and displacing opponents with her whip shot. I tend to switch to Brigitte when I am being heavily targeted while playing Ana, or when my fellow support selects Ana so that I can play a sort of body guard for her.

Brigitte's Abilities (Hover to See!)

Reapair Pack is Brigitte's way to heal her allies when Inspire is not active. It heals a teammate 110 health over 2 seconds. Since you only have 3 of these and they are fairly weak heals, it is all the more important that you keep Inspire active.

Whip Shot is a very basic ability. It allows Brigitte to swing her mace a long distance. It deals 75 damage, boops back the enemy, and triggers Inspire. Using this off cooldown can be effective, though sometimes you want to keep it up to play defense or dislodge an opponent from high ground.

Rally is Brigitte's ultimate. It is being changed significantly in Season 4, so I will give the updated stats instead of the current ones. Rally does several things. It gives the Brigitte player a 700 health shield, allows her shield bash to stun opponents, and gives her extra armor that regenerates. It also builds up 100 overhealth to allies that regenerates as long as Rally is active. It is a sustain ultimate that turns Brigitte into a miniature raid boss.

Brigitte's Pain Points (Click for More Info!)

  • Passivity
    • Brigitte will sometimes have to play extremely passive. Professional Brigitte players average around 25% Inspire uptime, which means they spend 75% of their time not passively healing their team!
    • Remember, you are more valuable alive with low healing than dead with no healing. Only proc Inspire when you are reasonably sure it is safe to do so.
  • Whip Shot Usage
    • Though whip shot is a simple ability, using it can be tricky. The most common use is simply to proc your Inspire.
    • Sometimes, though, countering a diving Winston/D.Va or booping away an ulting character is a more valuable target.
